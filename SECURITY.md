What "Supported" Means
:white_check_mark: Security updates and patches will be actively maintained for this version.
:x: Security updates and patches are no longer actively maintained for this version.

Reporting a Vulnerability
We take security seriously, and we appreciate your help in keeping our project secure. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to us following the guidelines below:
1.Reporting Process
a)Initial Report: Send an email to our security team at security@example.com with the subject line "Security Vulnerability Report."
b)Vulnerability Details: In your report, please include the following information:
  A detailed description of the vulnerability.
  Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
  The affected version(s) of our project.
  And any additional information that might help us bunderstand and fix the issue.
c)Assessment: Our security team will assess the reported vulnerability promptly. This assessment includes verifying the issue and determining its severity.
d)Response: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours and provide an initial assessment within 48 hours.
e)Fix and Disclosure: If the vulnerability is accepted, we will work on fixing it. Once fixed, we will follow responsible disclosure practices, coordinating with you to ensure the vulnerability is not disclosed prematurely.
f)Security Advisory: A security advisory will be issued once a fix is available. This advisory will provide details about the vulnerability, the affected versions, and instructions for users to update to the secure version.

Expectations
We appreciate responsible disclosure and ask that you do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had time to address it.
We will keep you informed of our progress throughout the process and will work with you to coordinate the release of information.
If a reported vulnerability is deemed not to be a security issue, we will provide an explanation.
Help Us Keep Our Project Secure
Your assistance in identifying and reporting security vulnerabilities is essential in maintaining the security of our project. We are committed to addressing and fixing security issues promptly to protect our users.

Thank you for your dedication to security and for helping us make our project safer for everyone.
